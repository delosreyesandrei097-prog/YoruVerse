About YoruVerse

YoruVerse is a community-driven reader and tracker for manhwa, manhua, and webtoons — built so you can discover, follow, and read your favorite series in one place.

What is YoruVerse?

YoruVerse is a free web platform that lets readers browse, search, bookmark, and read manhwa chapters through a single clean interface. Instead of jumping between scanlation sites, you get a unified library, reading progress sync, and update notifications across all of your devices.

Our goal is simple: give readers a fast, ad-light, mobile-friendly reader and give scanlation groups credit by always linking back to the original source.

What we provide

No Ads

Enjoy an uninterrupted reading experience with no banner ads, pop-ups, or distractions while browsing and reading your favorite manhwa.

Library & Bookmarks

Save series to your personal library, organize what you're reading vs. completed, and pick up exactly where you left off.

Reading History

Every chapter you open is recorded so you always know what you've read and what's next — synced to your account.

Favorites & Ratings

Heart the series you love, rate them, and help other readers discover great titles through the community ranking.

Comments & Discussion

Discuss chapters with other readers, share theories, and react — moderated to keep the conversation friendly.

Update Notifications

Auto-sync detects new chapters from your sources and notifies you the moment they're released.

EXP & Leaderboards

Earn experience for reading, commenting, and contributing. Climb the community leaderboard.

Installable PWA

Install YoruVerse to your home screen for an app-like experience with offline-ready shell.

Our purpose

YoruVerse exists to make following manhwa effortless: one library, one reading progress, one notification feed. We always credit the original scanlators and link back to their sites so readers can support them directly.

How content is stored

YoruVerse does not host or store chapter images, videos, or PDFs on its own servers. All chapter pages are streamed directly from the original scanlation sources. We only keep the URLs that point to those externally-hosted images.

Privacy Policy

What we collect

  • Account info you provide at sign-up: email, display name, and optional avatar.
  • Reading activity tied to your account: library, favorites, history, comments, ratings, EXP.
  • Basic technical data (browser/device type) used only to keep the app running.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers.
  • We do not store chapter images or video on our servers.
  • We do not run third-party tracking pixels or fingerprinting scripts.

How your data is protected

  • Authentication and database are handled by ■■■■■■■■ with security rules.
  • Only you (and administrators acting on your request) can modify your account data.
  • Comments are tied to your account and may be moderated to keep the community safe.

Your rights

  • You can update your profile and preferences at any time from Settings.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of update notifications without losing access to the rest of the app.
